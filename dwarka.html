Dwarka,this was my best ever trek because this time i was travelling with my bunch of friends inncluding my best friend.our journey started from rajkot we joined the group from rajkot.

waiting for bus

early morning we reached the dwarka and took breakfast and the menu was gujarati favourite batata pauva and chai.then we left for the shivrajpur to perform some adventure and the adventure was scuba diving.first of all scuba instructor gave us the instructions about how to communicate under the water and suddenly the scene of ZNMD film came in my mind. after the instruction they provided us scuba costume and we were ready to get the thrill of scuba.

all people turn by turn getting their turn and finally i got my turn.instructor told me to jump in the water and i did it and then he confirmed that i am okay and gettting proper oxygen or not.as i gave positive response and he did something and i found my self under the water ,surrounded by sea plants and different kind of fishesh. i was breathing in and breathing out try to experience all the things i was surrounded by.

with scuba costume

after coming back from scuba i was feeeling quite there were no thoughts,no feelings nothing just me and my blank mind.i sat for near about half an hour without talking anything.then we took lunch

and left for beyt dwarka iceland, there we pitched our tents and then photography started.

the most beautiful thing at beach is enjoying sunset and one should experience it once in a life.

Sunset at beyt-dwarka iceland

after dinner the camp fire has set and seating around camp fire and gossiping is the best part of trekkkking camp as all the people get chance to communicate with each other and have fun. after enjoying camp fire we took a walk along the bank of sea then we sat there with the group of people talking about their life story and eventuallly all were going silent and enjoying the light of full moon.we enjoyed moon-gazing untill 2 a.m. and then went to get some sleep although our heart didnt allow to go because of that beautifull and unforgettable moment.

Full moon at beyt dwarka iceland

next day early morning we took breakfast then packed our tents and left for dolphin exploration.

we took the boat ride for nearly 4-5 hours with full on DJ and how someone can stop their self when songs are playing around. we explored dolphins and took lunch at the middle of the sea.

after dolphin exploration we came back to dwarka and visited dwarkadhish temple,sudama setu and gomti ghat and how can we avoid the food exploration as we are fooody people.we explored the different type of dishesh and then finally reached to the bus and left to come back our home.

the most difficult part of journey is coming back.when you are returning to your home ,you have lot of memories and vibes which will make you emotionally weak.you will have new friends ,new bonds in just two-three days. this type of temporary journey gives you the parmenant people in your life.same thing happpened in our trip as wew weere leaving all the people got emotional and everyone was wishinng good bye to one another and that was the happy ending of our journey.
